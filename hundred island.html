Welcome to Hundred Islands

Hundred Islands National Park is a collection of 123 limestone islands off the coast of Pangasinan, Philippines. Known for its crystal-clear waters, diverse marine life, and lush greenery, it's a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Activities include island hopping, snorkeling, diving, kayaking, and zip-lining. Don't miss the breathtaking view from the Governor's Island viewpoint!

Hundred Islands National Park is located in the northern Philippines in the province of Pangasinan. There are 124 pristine coral reef islands scattered across an area of 7.12 square miles (18.44 sq km). The islands feature a coral reef foundation with the above ground cliffs being comprised of limestone.
